"Common Stock" means the common shares of the Company.
"Confidential Information" means any non-public information relating to the Company.
"Drag-Along Rights" means the right of a majority shareholder to force minority shareholders to join in the sale of the Company.
"Tag-Along Rights" means the right of minority shareholders to join in the sale initiated by majority shareholders.
"Shares" means the shares of stock issued by the Company.

4. Shareholder Rights and Obligations 
4.1 Voting Rights: Each Share shall entitle the holder to one vote on all matters submitted to shareholders.
4.2 Information Rights: Shareholders holding at least [Insert Percentage, e.g., 5%] of the Shares shall have the right to inspect the Company's books and records upon reasonable notice.

5. Transfer of Shares 
5.1 No Shareholder may transfer, sell, or encumber any Shares without the prior written consent of the Board and a majority of the other Shareholders.
5.2 Right of First Refusal: Any proposed transfer must first be offered to the Company and then to other Shareholders on the same terms.
5.3 Tag-Along Rights: If a Shareholder sells more than [Insert Percentage, e.g., 50%] of their Shares, other Shareholders may participate pro rata in the transaction.
5.4 Drag-Along Rights: If Shareholders holding at least [Insert Percentage, e.g., 75%] of the Shares approve a sale of the Company, all Shareholders must consent to the sale.
5.5 Transfers in violation of this Section shall be null and void.
