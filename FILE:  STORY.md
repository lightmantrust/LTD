# The Story of LightMan Digital Trust

> “We started with a flashlight and a notebook.”  
> — [lightman.log](lightman.log) block 0

In 2021 a power outage in Puerto Rico left 3.2 million people without electricity—and without trust in the centralised outage-reporting portal.  
A solo maintainer (@lightman) hacked together a solar-powered Raspberry Pi that logged neighbour-reported outages on a hand-drawn Merkle tree.  
That notebook became the genesis block you see today.

Read the full 3-minute story at [story.lightman-trust.io](https://story.lightman-trust.io) (audio NFT available).

## Moral
If you can shine a light, you can keep a ledger.
